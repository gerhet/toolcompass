How to use the Tip & Bill Splitter

Dining out with friends or colleagues shouldn't end with awkward math at the table. Our Tip & Bill Splitter makes it easy to divide restaurant checks fairly among everyone. Simply enter the total bill amount, select your desired tip percentage, and specify the number of people sharing the cost.

The calculator instantly shows how much each person owes, including a detailed breakdown of the subtotal, tip amount, and total. You can copy the result to share in group chats or payment apps like Venmo, PayPal, or Splitwise.

In the United States, 15-18% is considered standard for good restaurant service. 20% or more is appreciated for exceptional service. For basic service, 10% is acceptable.

Etiquette experts recommend calculating tip on the pre-tax subtotal. However, many people tip on the total amount for simplicity. Our calculator uses the bill amount you enter.

This calculator splits evenly among all people. For uneven splits (different orders), you may need to calculate individual amounts separately or use a dedicated app.
