How to use X-IDENTITY Engine

The X-IDENTITY Engine uses advanced linguistic algorithms combined with genre-specific databases to generate unique, memorable names. Whether you're creating characters for a novel, building a brand identity, or looking for the perfect gamertag, our tool delivers instant inspiration.
