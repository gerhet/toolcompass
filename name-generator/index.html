The X-IDENTITY Engine is designed to bridge the gap between imagination and reality. By combining linguistic algorithms with genre-specific databases, we provide instant inspiration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these names copyright free? ▼

Yes, the generated names are algorithmic combinations and are free to use for your projects. However, always check trademark databases for commercial use.

Does the tool save my history? ▼

We do not store data on our servers. However, you can use the "Vault" feature to save favorites locally in your browser's storage.

Can I generate names offline? ▼

Once the page is loaded, all calculations happen in your browser. You can disconnect from the internet and the tool will continue to work.
