Why Your Social Media Bio Matters

Your social media bio is often the first impression people have of you. A well-crafted bio can attract followers, build credibility, and communicate your unique value proposition in seconds. Whether you're building a personal brand or promoting a business, your bio should be clear, engaging, and authentic.

Platform Character Limits

  • Instagram: 150 characters – Keep it punchy and include a call-to-action.
  • Twitter / X: 160 characters – Be concise and use relevant keywords.
  • LinkedIn: 220 characters – Focus on professional value and expertise.
  • TikTok: 80 characters – Short, fun, and personality-driven.
