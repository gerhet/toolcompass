Master Your Mortgage with Precision

Buying a home is likely the largest financial transaction of your life. Understanding exactly how your monthly payments are split between principal and interest is crucial for long-term financial health. The Mortgage Splitter provides a real-time, visual breakdown of your loan amortization.

Pro tips: Use the sliders to see how extra payments or rate changes affect your timeline. A difference of just 0.5% in interest can save you over $30,000 on a 30-year loan.

The monthly payment is calculated using the standard amortization formula, factoring in the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term.
