Understanding Your Mortgage Repayments

Taking out a mortgage is one of the biggest financial decisions you'll ever make. This Mortgage Splitter helps you break down the numbers to see exactly where your money goes. When you pay off a loan, your monthly installment consists of two main parts: the principal (the amount you borrowed) and the interest (what the bank charges you).

How Interest Rates Impact Your Loan

Even a 1% difference in your interest rate can save you tens of thousands of dollars over the lifespan of a 30-year loan. For example, on a $300,000 mortgage, paying 4% instead of 5% interest can save you over $60,000 in total interest costs.
