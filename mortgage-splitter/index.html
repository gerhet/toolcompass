Master Your Mortgage with Precision

Buying a home is likely the largest financial transaction of your life. Understanding exactly how your monthly payments are split between principal and interest is crucial for long-term financial health. The Mortgage Splitter provides a real-time, visual breakdown of your loan amortization.

How Interest Rates Impact Your Wallet

Interest rates might seem like small percentages, but over a 30-year term, they compound significantly. A difference of just 0.5% in your interest rate on a $300,000 loan can result in a difference of over $30,000 in total interest paid over the life of the loan.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Instant Feedback: Adjust sliders to see how extra payments or rate changes affect your timeline.
  • Privacy First: Unlike other calculators, we don't send your financial data to a server. Everything happens in your browser.
  • Visual Clarity: See the ratio of Principal vs. Interest at a glance with our dynamic gold visualization.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this include taxes?

No, this calculator focuses on Principal and Interest (P&I). Property taxes and insurance (PITI) vary by location and should be added separately to your budget.

Is my data saved?

Your inputs are saved locally in your browser's LocalStorage so they remain when you return, but they are never sent to our servers.

Can I export the data?

Yes, click the "Export CSV" button in the tool header to download a spreadsheet of your amortization schedule.
