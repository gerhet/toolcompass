How the Age Calculator Works

This free age calculator computes your exact age down to the second by comparing your birth date to the current date and time. Unlike a simple subtraction, it correctly handles leap years, months of different lengths, and whether your birthday has already passed this year — giving you a precise result every time.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your birth date is never transmitted to any server, never stored in a database, and no account is required. If you use the Save feature, results are kept in your browser's own localStorage and can be deleted whenever you like.

Note: Life statistics (heartbeats, breaths, hours slept) are estimates based on population averages and are intended for fun only. They are not medical measurements. Consult a healthcare professional for any health-related questions.

What the Calculator Shows

  • Exact age — years, total months, and total days since your birth date.
  • Live timer — a real-time counter of years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds, updating every second.
  • Next birthday countdown — days remaining until your next birthday, with the exact date.
  • Zodiac sign — your Western astrological sign based on birth month and day.
  • Life statistics — estimated heartbeats, breaths, and hours slept since birth.
  • Day of the week — the weekday you were born on.

Frequently Asked Questions
