Why Compare Travel Emissions?

Transportation is responsible for approximately 24% of direct CO2 emissions from fuel combustion. While flying is often the fastest way to travel, its climate impact is disproportionately high. Our CO2 Comparator helps you visualize the environmental cost of your journey, using weighted averages for modern vehicle fleets.

The Science Behind the Numbers

We use standardized emission factors (kg CO2 per passenger kilometer): Train (0.03), Car (0.18), and Aviation (0.25). The aviation factor includes radiative forcing, which accounts for non-CO2 climate impacts like contrails and nitrogen oxides at high altitudes.

Electric vehicle (EV) emissions depend heavily on the local power grid's energy mix. While this calculator uses a petrol car average (0.18kg/km), an EV on a renewable grid can drop below 0.05kg/km.
