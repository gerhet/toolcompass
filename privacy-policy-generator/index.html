How to use the Privacy Policy Generator

A privacy policy is legally required for most websites and apps that collect any user data. Our Privacy Policy Generator creates comprehensive, compliant policies tailored to your specific business needs. Simply fill in your business information, select what data you collect, and generate your policy instantly.

The generated policy covers all major requirements including GDPR (European Union), CCPA (California), and CalOPPA (California Online Privacy Protection Act). You can customize every section and download in multiple formats for easy integration into your website or app.

Important: While our generator creates compliant templates, we recommend having a legal professional review your specific situation, especially for complex businesses or sensitive data handling.
