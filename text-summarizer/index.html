    How our Text Summarizer Works

    In the age of information overload, being able to quickly grasp the core message of a text is vital. Our AI Text Summarizer uses an extractive summarization algorithm to identify the most significant sentences in your document based on word frequency, sentence position, and semantic importance.

    Simply paste your text, choose your desired summary length (3, 5, or 7 sentences), and get the key points instantly. The tool shows you exactly how much time you've saved with reduction percentage and reading time comparisons.

    Whether you're a student reviewing research papers, a professional scanning industry reports, or a content creator needing quick abstracts, this tool helps you process information faster without missing important details.

    Our extractive summarization identifies the most important sentences based on multiple factors. While not perfect for all text types, it works well for articles, reports, and informational content. For best results, use texts between 500-5000 words.
