How much of 2026 is gone?

Our Year Progress Tracker provides a real-time visualization of the current year. Using high-precision calculations, we show you exactly how much of the year has passed, down to the fourth decimal point. This tool is designed for professionals, students, and anyone practicing Memento Mori or time-blocking techniques.

Why track year percentage?

Time awareness is the first step toward high productivity. Whether you are tracking financial quarters (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4), monitoring project deadlines, or simply reflecting on personal goals, seeing the year as a progress bar helps put time into perspective.

This tool is 100% private. All calculations happen locally in your browser. No data is stored, making it the most secure time tracking utility on the web.
