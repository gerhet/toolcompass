How much of 2026 has passed?

Our real-time Year Progress Tracker calculates the exact percentage of 2026 that has elapsed — updated every second. It's a simple but powerful way to visualize time as a finite resource. Whether you're a freelancer tracking quarterly goals, a student monitoring academic progress, or simply someone who wants to stay intentional with their year — this tool delivers clarity at a glance.

The tracker shows your current day of the year, ISO week number, active quarter with a live progress bar, a month-by-month overview, and a full day grid of all 365 days. Elapsed days are highlighted in gold, today's date pulses in cyan, and remaining days are shown in grey — giving you an honest, visual picture of where your year stands.

The Year Progress Tracker automatically calculates and displays today's day of the year (day 1–365) updated in real time. You can see it in the "Days gone" stat card above.

The current ISO week number is displayed live in the "Week no." stat card. It updates automatically at midnight each Monday.

We calculate the milliseconds elapsed since January 1st 00:00:00 divided by the total milliseconds in the year. This gives a high-precision percentage updated every second — not just once per day.
