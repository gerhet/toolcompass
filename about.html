About ToolCompass

ToolCompass is a curated collection of free, browser-based productivity tools designed to help developers, creators, freelancers, and professionals work smarter — without signups, installations, or hidden costs.

🎯 Why ToolCompass Exists

We noticed that many everyday tasks — from calculating VAT to formatting JSON, generating secure passwords, or estimating reading time — require switching between multiple websites, dealing with intrusive ads, or installing bloated software.

ToolCompass solves this by offering:

  • ✅ 100% free tools — no paywalls, no premium tiers
  • ✅ No registration required — just open and use instantly
  • ✅ Privacy-first design — all calculations run locally in your browser; we never store your inputs
  • ✅ Lightweight & fast — optimized for performance, even on slower connections
  • ✅ Open & transparent — built with care, hosted reliably on GitHub Pages

🔧 How We Build Our Tools

Every tool on ToolCompass is carefully designed to be accurate, intuitive, and distraction-free. We prioritize reliability over quantity. If a tool doesn’t meet our standards for usability and correctness, we don’t publish it. All tools are regularly tested and updated to ensure they work across modern browsers and devices.

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

ToolCompass processes all data locally in your browser. This means your inputs never leave your device, and we don’t collect, track, or sell any personal information. Our Privacy Policy explains exactly how we handle the minimal data required to keep the site running.

🪧 A Note About Ads

ToolCompass is and will always be free to use. To cover domain, hosting, and maintenance costs, we display non-intrusive ads via Google AdSense.

What this means for you:

  • 🚫 No pop-ups, auto-play videos, or misleading ad buttons
  • 🚫 No cross-site tracking beyond standard ad network requirements
  • ✅ Ads are clearly labeled and visually separated from tool content
  • ✅ You can use ad-blockers — we respect your choice (though your support helps us keep the site free!)

Transparency note: If you click on an ad and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This directly funds tool improvements and server costs.
