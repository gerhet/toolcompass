Why use a QR Code?

QR codes (Quick Response codes) have become an essential bridge between the physical and digital worlds. Whether you want to share a website link, a Wi-Fi password, or digital contact information, a QR code makes the process seamless for mobile users.

Safe and Private Generation

At ToolCompass, we prioritize your privacy. Our QR Code Generator works entirely within your browser. The data you enter is never sent to our servers, ensuring that your sensitive information remains yours alone.

Best Practices for QR Codes

  • Contrast: Ensure your QR code is dark on a light background for better scannability.
  • Size: Don't make the code too small; a minimum of 2x2 cm is recommended for print.
  • Testing: Always scan your generated code with your own smartphone before printing it on business cards or flyers.
