How to use the QR Code Generator

Our Free QR Code Generator creates high-quality, customizable QR codes for any purpose. Whether you need a QR code for your website, WiFi network, business card, or marketing materials, this tool provides professional results with full customization options.

Simply select your QR code type (URL, Text, WiFi, Email, Phone, or SMS), enter your content, customize colors and size, optionally add your logo, and click generate. Your QR code is created instantly in your browser and ready to download in multiple formats.

Best practices for QR codes: Ensure high contrast between foreground and background colors, use minimum 2x2cm size for print, test before mass printing, and keep error correction level high when adding logos.

Privacy guarantee: All QR code generation happens entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your input data. Your URLs and content stay on your device.
