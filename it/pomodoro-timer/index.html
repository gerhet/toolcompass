How to use the Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro Technique is a proven time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo. Work in focused 25-minute sessions (called "pomodoros"), followed by 5-minute breaks. After completing 4 pomodoros, take a longer 15-30 minute break.

Our timer makes it easy to follow this method. Select your mode (Work, Short Break, or Long Break), click Start, and focus on your task. When the timer ends, you'll hear a notification sound. Track your completed pomodoros in the statistics section to stay motivated.

Pro tips: Eliminate distractions during work sessions, use breaks to physically move around, and customize timer durations to match your personal productivity rhythm.
