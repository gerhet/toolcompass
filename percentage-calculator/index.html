How to use the Percentage Calculator

Our Percentage Calculator handles all common percentage calculations in one place. Whether you need to find a percentage of a value, calculate percentage change, or determine what percentage one number is of another, this tool provides instant, accurate results.

Simply select the calculation type you need, enter your values, and see results instantly. Each calculation includes a visual progress bar and the formula used, helping you understand the math behind the result. Your calculation history is saved automatically for quick reference.

Common uses: Calculate discounts, tips, tax amounts, salary increases, grade percentages, investment returns, and more. Perfect for students, professionals, and everyday calculations.

Privacy guarantee: All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your input data. Your numbers stay on your device.

To calculate percentage, divide the part by the whole and multiply by 100. For example, 50 out of 250 is (50/250) × 100 = 20%. Our calculator does this automatically.

Percentage change = ((New Value - Original Value) / Original Value) × 100. A positive result means increase, negative means decrease.
