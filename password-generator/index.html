Password Security Tips Best Practices
  • Use at least 16 characters for important accounts (email, banking)
  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Never reuse passwords across different accounts
  • Use a password manager to store your passwords securely
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
  • Change passwords immediately if a service reports a breach

How to use the Password Generator

Our Secure Password Generator creates strong, random passwords that are nearly impossible to crack. Simply adjust the length slider, select which character types to include, and click generate. Your password is created instantly in your browser.

Why use strong passwords? Weak passwords are the #1 cause of hacked accounts. A strong password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Our generator creates passwords that meet all security best practices.

Important: Always copy your generated password to a secure password manager immediately. Never store passwords in plain text files or emails.
