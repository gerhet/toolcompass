How to use the Stopwatch

Our Precision Stopwatch offers millisecond-accurate time tracking directly in your browser. Whether you're timing sports activities, work sessions, cooking, or any other task, this tool provides reliable measurements with lap time functionality.

Click Start to begin timing, Lap to record split times without stopping, and Reset to clear everything. Your lap times are automatically saved and can be exported as CSV for use in spreadsheets or shared with others.

Keyboard shortcuts make hands-free operation easy: Spacebar starts/stops, L records a lap, and R resets. Perfect for when your hands are busy or you need quick control.

Privacy guarantee: All stopwatch data stays entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your timing information. Your lap times remain private on your device.
