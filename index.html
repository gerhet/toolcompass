Why we built ToolCompass

Most online tool sites make you register, show you intrusive popups, or send your data to a server to process it. We wanted something different: a clean, fast collection of utilities that respect your privacy and just work.

ToolCompass was created by a small team of developers and designers who were tired of switching between 15 different bookmarks to do simple calculations. Everything here is purpose-built to be as fast as opening a calculator app — but richer and more useful. Here are some of the most popular tools people use every day:

  • VAT Calculator — add or strip tax at any rate
  • Password Generator — strong, random, private
  • JSON Formatter — prettify messy API output
  • Pomodoro Timer — structured deep focus sessions
  • Word Counter — real-time count with reading time
  • BMI Calculator — metric and imperial, no login
  • Hourly Rate Calculator — set fair freelance prices
  • QR Code Generator — any URL or WiFi in seconds

New tools are added regularly. If you have a suggestion or find a bug, please reach out via the About page. We read every message.
