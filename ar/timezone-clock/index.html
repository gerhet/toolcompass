How to use the Global Timezone Clock

Managing a remote team or working with international clients requires precise time coordination. Our Global Timezone Clock displays live time for multiple cities simultaneously, updating every second. Whether you're scheduling meetings across continents or tracking office hours for global branches, this tool gives you instant clarity.

Add your preferred cities using the dropdown or quick-add buttons. Each clock shows the current time, business hours status (open/closed), and day/night indicator. Your selections are saved automatically so they're ready when you return. The meeting planner suggests optimal times for cross-timezone calls.
