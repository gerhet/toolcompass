How to use the VAT Calculator

Our VAT Calculator is designed for freelancers, business owners, and consumers who need quick, accurate tax calculations without complex spreadsheets. Whether you are invoicing a client or checking a receipt, this tool handles the math instantly in your browser.

Simply enter the net amount and your local VAT rate (e.g., 19% in Germany, 20% in UK). Click "Add VAT" to get the gross price, or "Remove VAT" if you have a total receipt and need to find the net value for expense reports.

To add VAT, multiply the price by (1 + VAT rate). For example, €100 × 1.19 = €119. To remove VAT, divide the total by (1 + VAT rate). For example, €119 / 1.19 = €100.
