How to Use the VAT Calculator

Our VAT Calculator is designed for freelancers, business owners, and consumers who need quick, accurate tax calculations without complex spreadsheets. Whether you are invoicing a client or checking a receipt, this tool handles the math instantly.

Adding VAT (Net to Gross)

To calculate the final price including tax, enter the net amount and your local VAT rate. The tool applies the standard formula:

Gross Price = Net Price × (1 + VAT Rate / 100)

For example, a €100 product with 19% VAT becomes €119.00.

Removing VAT (Gross to Net)

If you have a total price and need to find the original net amount (e.g., for expense reports), select "Remove VAT". The logic reverses the calculation:

Net Price = Gross Price / (1 + VAT Rate / 100)
