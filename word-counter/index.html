Why reading time matters

Average adult reading speed is around 200 words per minute for online content. Knowing your estimated reading time helps you match your content length to your audience's attention span. Blog posts under 7 minutes tend to perform better for engagement; long-form content above 10 minutes tends to rank better in SEO.

Simply paste or type your text into the box above. Words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and reading time are all calculated instantly — no button required, no account, no data sent anywhere.

Keyword density shows how often specific words appear relative to your total word count. For SEO, a density of 1–3% for your target keyword is generally considered healthy — too low and the topic isn't clear, too high and it looks like keyword stuffing.

The "Characters" stat includes spaces. The "No Spaces" stat excludes them. Most social media platforms (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram) count spaces in their character limits.

Reading time is estimated by dividing the word count by 200 (the average adult online reading speed in words per minute). A 1,000-word article will show approximately 5 minutes reading time.
