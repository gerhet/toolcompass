Why Use a Color Palette Generator?

Finding the perfect color combination for your design project can be time-consuming. Our random color palette generator helps you discover beautiful color schemes instantly. Whether you're designing a website, creating a brand identity, or working on digital art, having a cohesive color palette is essential.

Key Features

  • Instant Generation: Create unlimited color palettes with a single click or spacebar press.
  • Lock Colors: Found a color you love? Lock it and regenerate the rest until you find the perfect combination.
  • Save Favorites: Save your favorite palettes locally in your browser for easy access later.
  • Export Options: Export palettes to CSS, SCSS, or JSON format for easy integration into your projects.
  • Copy to Clipboard: Click any color to copy its HEX code instantly.
