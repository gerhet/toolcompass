Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Protection at a Glance

ToolCompass.org is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our visitors on our servers. All calculations and data inputs within our tools happen locally in your browser (client-side).

2. Hosting & Server Log Files

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages (or Vercel). When you visit our site, the hosting provider automatically collects and stores information in "server log files," which your browser transmits automatically. This includes IP addresses, browser type, and time of request. This data is used solely for technical security and stability and is not merged with other data sources.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Currently, we do not use tracking cookies, analytics (like Google Analytics), or marketing scripts. If we implement advertising services (like Google AdSense) in the future, we will update this policy and provide a consent banner according to law.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not store any personal data, we cannot provide information about your data, nor can we delete it, as it never reaches our systems. You have the right to contact the relevant data protection authorities if you have concerns about the hosting provider's data handling.
