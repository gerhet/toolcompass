How to calculate working days between dates

Calculating net working days between two dates is essential for accurate project planning, resource allocation, and deadline estimation. Our Workday Counter excludes weekends (Saturday and Sunday) automatically and converts your timeline into actionable metrics: total business days, work hours, and even down to the minute.

💡 Pro Tip: For more precise planning, manually subtract public holidays from the result. Pair this tool with our Pomodoro Timer to break down your working days into focused productivity sessions.

Whether you're a project manager estimating sprint capacity, a freelancer quoting a deadline, or a student planning study schedules — this tool provides surgical precision. Simply select your start and end date, and the calculator instantly shows you how many Monday-to-Friday workdays fall within that range, along with the equivalent hours (based on an 8-hour workday standard).

All calculations run locally in your browser using JavaScript. This means your date inputs never leave your device, and the tool works even with limited internet connectivity. Note: This tool is for estimation purposes only. It does not account for public holidays, company-specific schedules, or regional variations. For legal or payroll calculations, please consult official resources.

We multiply the net working days by 8 (standard workday). For example, 10 working days = 80 work hours. If your workday differs (e.g. 7.5h or 9h), simply adjust the result proportionally.
